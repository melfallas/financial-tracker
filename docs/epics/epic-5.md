# Epic 5: Consultation Booking & Scheduling

**Goal:** Enable the final conversion step — scheduling a consultation with a financial advisor, with full email automation. ⭐ _Critical for lead conversion._

## Context
Provide the final step of the funnel flow - allowing a captured lead to book a consultation through an external calendar integration, with relevant confirmation emails. 

## Stories Include:
- US5.1: Floating CTA Button
- US5.2: Booking Screen & Calendar Integration
- US5.3: Booking Email Automation
