## Usage Guidelines

### General Principles

- Moderation is key: avoid using all colors with the same intensity.
- To maintain elegance, apply a hierarchy where **Deep Blue (#1A3C6E)** and **Cool Gray (#ECEFF1)** dominate the space.
- Bright colors should be reserved for **interaction highlights** and **specific calls to action**.
- Follow the **80/15/5 rule**:
  - **80%** of the page should use Deep Blue and Cool Gray.
  - **15%** should be Charcoal text and Teal icons.
  - **5%** should be vibrant colors (Green, Orange, Red) for CTAs and alerts.

---

### Color Palette by Elements

#### 1. Structure and Backgrounds

- **Main Background:** `#ECEFF1` (Cool Gray) → softer than pure white, conveys professionalism.
- **Hero Section Background:** `#1A3C6E` (Deep Blue) → strong first impression, authority.
- **Top Menu:** `#1A3C6E` (background) or `#37474F` (text on light backgrounds).

#### 2. Typography

- **Headings (H1, H2):** `#1A3C6E` (Deep Blue).
- **Body Text:** `#37474F` (Charcoal).
- **Text on dark backgrounds:** `#ECEFF1` (Cool Gray) or White.

#### 3. Buttons and CTAs

- **Primary Button:** `#00C853` (Bright Emerald Green) → main conversion actions like “Hire Advisory” or “Start Diagnosis.”
- **Secondary Button:** `#F57C00` (Warm Orange) → actions like “Learn More” or “Sign In.”
- **Ghost Buttons:** outlined with `#1A3C6E`.

#### 4. Icons and Visual Elements

- **Benefit Icons:** `#009688` (Teal) → refined, fintech-oriented.
- **Financial Health Indicators (Traffic Light):**
  - `#D32F2F` (Red) → debts, critical alerts.
  - `#FFC107` (Amber) → warnings, preventive tips.
  - `#00C853` (Green) → goals achieved, positive outcomes.

---

## 💡 Golden Rule for Elegance

To avoid a “gaming app” look, apply the **80/15/5 proportion**:

- **80%** → Cool Gray + Deep Blue.
- **15%** → Charcoal + Teal.
- **5%** → Emerald Green, Orange, Red (only for CTAs and alerts).

---

## Using Proven Structure

This approach balances **institutional elegance** with **modern fintech appeal**.  
By applying a tested layout structure and injecting this color hierarchy, the platform achieves both credibility and innovation.

### Applied Design Concept

1. **General Background:** `#ECEFF1` (Cool Gray) → clean, luminous, professional.
2. **Top Menu:** Deep Blue links, Charcoal text. Corporate seriousness.
3. **Hero Section:**
   - Title in Deep Blue.
   - Highlight in Emerald Green (`#00C853`) → conveys growth and wealth.
   - Subtext in Charcoal for readability.
4. **Primary CTA Button:** Emerald Green (`#00C853`) → the most vibrant element, guiding users toward financial health actions.
