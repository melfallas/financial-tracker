# Epic 2: Lead Capture, PDF Generation & Email Delivery

**Goal:** Deliver the complete lead capture funnel — form validation, local persistence, client-side PDF report generation, and automated email delivery. **This is the highest business-value delivery.**

## Context
This epic provides the lead capture functionality, starting from user forms through to automated email delivery of personalized financial analysis PDF reports.

## Stories Include:
- US2.1: Lead Capture Form Component
- US2.2: PDF Report Generation
- US2.3: PDF Email Delivery
- US2.4: Lead Form Success Screen
