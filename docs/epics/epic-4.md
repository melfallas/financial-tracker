# Epic 4: Market Intelligence & Awareness Widgets

**Goal:** Implement the "hook" layer — emotionally compelling widgets that generate urgency and need.

## Context
Features designed to show users the real-world impact of wait times, provide market context (fear & greed, specific rate differentials), and generally provide urgency elements for the conversion flow.

## Stories Include:
- US4.1: Hero Section & Navbar
- US4.2: Cost of Waiting Banner & Financial Freedom Countdown
- US4.3: Currency Comparison & Market Status Widgets
